About Us:

Founded in 2006, Modern Music Express has grown from a small, specialist store to Ireland's largest online music store with a vibrant and growing community of music lovers. MME's online store contains one of the largest catalogue of CDs, Vinyls and music DVDs.

Our mission is to provided you, the customer, with access to an unrivalled collection of music, encompassing multiple genres, all at competitive prices.

In addition to our online store, we have an active and ever growing community section. Here, you can post your music reviews and recommendations for other users to view!
